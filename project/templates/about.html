Welcome to Canine Care


At Canine Care, every dog receives loving care, even when you're away. Founded by ardent dog lovers, Canine Care bridges the gap between absent pet owners and enthusiastic pet sitters in your local community. At Canine Care, we understand that your dog is more than just an animal - they're a beloved member of your family.


Our Mission is simple

To provide a safe, reliable, and caring environment for your dog. We're wild about making pet care easy, accessible, and stress-free for pet owners in every corner of the community. Whether you need a last-minute sitter for a quick work-trip, or a reliable buddy for your furry friend during your holidays, Canine Care is here to help!
